Introduction

The advanced pipe spacing calculator is intended for piping engineers and designers who need a quick, reliable way to establish minimum spacing for parallel pipelines. It focuses on practical, real-world spacing decisions so you get a clear, engineering-oriented results which update instantly.

Use the tool to model multiple pipeline arrangements, estimate the total space occupied by the pipelines and export the results as DXF drawing or a compact PDF report, to further use in your studies. Inputs such as NPS, flange class and insulation thickness are used to generate geometry that you can verify visually and export for drafting.

For background on the calculation assumptions (staggered flanges, rounding rules and verification layers), examples of DXF output, sample figures and a detailed user guide, visit our Help page.
